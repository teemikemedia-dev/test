FAQ

Free Website Audit FAQs

Common questions about the free website audit and how it helps identify SEO, UX, trust, and conversion improvement opportunities.

What does the free website audit review?

The audit reviews practical areas such as first impression, website structure, mobile experience, SEO foundations, trust signals, calls to action, and conversion flow.

Do I need to include https:// in my website link?

No. You can enter your website with or without https://. The form is designed to accept normal website links such as yourwebsite.com.

Is the website audit really free?

Yes. The audit request is free. It gives you a clearer starting point for understanding what may be affecting trust, visibility, mobile usability, or lead generation.

What happens after I submit my website for audit?

You will receive a confirmation, and the website can be reviewed for key improvement areas. If needed, we can follow up with recommendations for design, redesign, SEO, or conversion improvements.

Can a website audit help with SEO?

Yes. A website audit can reveal SEO issues such as weak metadata, poor heading structure, missing alt text, thin content, unclear page hierarchy, and internal linking gaps.

Can the audit show why my website is not getting leads?

Yes. The audit can identify common lead-generation problems such as unclear messaging, weak calls to action, poor mobile flow, missing trust signals, or confusing navigation.

Do you audit mobile website experience?

Yes. Mobile experience is reviewed because many visitors browse on phones, and poor spacing, hard-to-read text, or hidden calls to action can reduce inquiries.

Can I request an audit before a website redesign?

Yes. A free website audit is a smart first step before redesigning because it helps clarify what should be fixed, improved, removed, or rebuilt.

Will the audit include conversion recommendations?

Yes. The audit focuses on practical conversion areas such as page flow, trust sections, calls to action, contact paths, and friction points.

Can I submit any type of website for audit?

Yes. You can submit business websites, service websites, ecommerce stores, WordPress sites, Wix sites, Shopify stores, course websites, and other professional websites.
