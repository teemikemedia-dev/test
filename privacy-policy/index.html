Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Teemikemedia Agency Ltd. collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you visit our website or contact us about a project.

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Quick Summary

We only collect information needed to respond to inquiries, deliver our services, improve our website, and maintain secure business communication.

Contact Us

1. Who We Are

Teemikemedia Agency Ltd. is a website design and digital services agency. In this policy, “Teemikemedia Agency,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Teemikemedia Agency Ltd.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, phone number, business name, website URL, project details, consultation notes, and messages sent through our forms, email, WhatsApp, Calendly, or live chat.

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, referral source, and website usage data through analytics, cookies, pixels, or similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, prepare project recommendations, book consultations, deliver services, provide support, send project updates, improve our website, measure marketing performance, prevent abuse, and meet legal or administrative obligations.

4. Cookies And Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tools to improve site performance, remember preferences, understand visitor behavior, support advertising, and measure campaign results. You can adjust cookie settings through your browser at any time.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us run our business, such as hosting providers, analytics platforms, CRM tools, payment processors, scheduling tools, communication platforms, and professional advisers.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, to prevent fraud, or to respond to valid legal requests.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may include tools or links from third parties such as Calendly, Google, WhatsApp, Tawk.to, social media platforms, and review platforms. Their services are governed by their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them when using those tools.

7. Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, manage projects, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve our services.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain uses of your personal information. You may also request that we stop sending marketing messages.

To make a privacy request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before completing certain requests.

10. International Visitors

If you access our website from outside the country where our systems or providers operate, your information may be processed in other locations. We take reasonable steps to handle personal information consistently with this policy.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for businesses and individuals seeking professional digital services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page. Continued use of our website means you accept the updated policy.
