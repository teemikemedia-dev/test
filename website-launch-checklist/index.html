What To Check Before Your Website Goes Live

Use these checkpoints to avoid broken forms, weak metadata, missing analytics, mobile issues, and launch-day surprises.

Content Review

Make sure visitors can understand your business quickly.

  • Final copy
  • No placeholder text
  • Clear CTAs
  • Proofread pages

Links & Navigation

Check every path a visitor may use.

  • Menu links
  • Footer links
  • Button links
  • Internal pages

Forms

Test every form before launch.

  • Contact form
  • Audit form
  • Success messages
  • Email delivery

SEO Setup

Review search basics on every important page.

  • Page titles
  • Meta descriptions
  • Canonical URLs
  • Sitemap and robots

Tracking

Make sure performance can be measured after launch.

  • Google Analytics
  • Search Console
  • Verification tags
  • Conversion paths

Mobile QA

Review mobile before desktop approval.

  • Readable text
  • Working buttons
  • No overlaps
  • Fast contact access
